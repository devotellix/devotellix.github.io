Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Devotellix, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage for the AeroRatio iOS application.

1. 100% Offline-First Architecture

AeroRatio is designed as an offline-first app. Your paint calculations, recipe journal, and custom paint profiles are stored locally on your device using SwiftData and are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Collection & Analytics

We believe in minimal data collection. We do NOT collect personal data, email addresses, or account credentials. AeroRatio uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage telemetry to help us improve the app and audit paint database usage.

  • Telemetry is completely anonymous and does not include any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We track events like app launches, paint searches, and preference changes (e.g. changing units or language).
  • You can easily opt out of this anonymous analytics telemetry at any time in the app's Settings under the TelemetryDeck section.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising SDKs, or third-party marketing frameworks (such as Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, or AdMob).

4. Paid App Purchase

AeroRatio is a paid upfront application with zero subscription fees, ads, or features hidden behind paywalls. All transactions are securely processed by Apple via the App Store, and we do not have access to your billing details or payment information.

5. Your Rights

Because we do not store your personal data, run user accounts, or maintain cloud databases, you have complete control over your data. You can delete all your stored recipes and profiles simply by uninstalling the application from your iOS device.
