Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where is my project data stored? Everything is saved 100% locally on your iOS device via Apple's native SwiftData framework. We have no access to your project databases.
  • Does the Labor Timer work in background? Yes. The timer is designed specifically to run in the background. It also implements Apple's Live Activities API to show you your active session cost accumulating directly on your Lock Screen.
  • Are there any monthly subscription fees? No. CraftWorth is a simple one-time purchase of €2.99 on the App Store with no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or ads.
