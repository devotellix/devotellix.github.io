An Editorial Journal, Native to iOS

Built with generous breathing room and warm tinted neutrals for quiet reflection.

On-Device Privacy First

No databases, trackers, or advertising scripts. All logs stay strictly on your local hardware using Apple's secure database framework, private by design.

Guided Reflection Prompts

Choose from pre-designed templates for morning gratitude, self-growth, travel memories, relationships, and monthly life reviews to kickstart your writing.

On-Device Insights

Discover emotional patterns, mood trends, and energy spikes with local charting. Visualize your life rhythms without sending diagnostic data to servers.

Calendar & Visual Moments

Browse your entries using a flexible calendar view, a grid of visual photo cards, or filter by collections, tags, and places you have visited.

Data Sovereignty (JSON)

Your memories belong to you forever. Export your entire journal timeline in a readable, standard JSON format at any time with a single tap in settings.

Open Backup & Export

Export your journal as an open JSON file and your guided life story as a styled PDF. Your data stays portable without an account or proprietary cloud.

Interactive Reflection Builder

Simulate writing a memory card. Select a prompt, set your mood, write a note, and see the local rendering generated by the app.
