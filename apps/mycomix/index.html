Sterilization Reference

Grain Spawn (Millet/Oats) 90 - 120 mins

Sterilize at 15 PSI (121°C) to ensure endospore elimination.

Agar Media (Petri Dishes) 30 - 45 mins

Sterilize at 15 PSI (121°C). Avoid over-caramelizing sugars.

Liquid Culture (Syringes/Jars) 20 - 30 mins

Sterilize at 15 PSI. Swirl gently after cooling to check clarity.
