Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

TL;DR: MycoMix respects your privacy. We run completely offline, do not require registration, and collect zero personal information, formulation details, or growth metrics. Everything stays on your device.

1. Offline-First Architecture

MycoMix is built to run entirely offline on your iOS device. All substrate calculations, monotub designs, custom recipe storage, and timers are processed locally using Apple's SwiftData. We do not run backend servers, and we never sync, transmit, or sell your data.

2. Permissions

The application may ask for the following standard iOS permissions depending on how you use it:

  • Notifications: Used strictly to alert you when your sterilization, pasteurization, or cooking timers expire. You can decline this permission and still use all calculators and recipe databases.

3. Local Storage and Backups

Your saved calculations, custom monotub shapes, and personalized recipes are stored securely in your device's sandbox. If you back up your iOS device via Apple iCloud, your data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account in accordance with Apple's standard security policies. We do not have access to these backups.

4. Analytics and Crash Logs

To keep MycoMix stable and bug-free, we utilize Apple's standard diagnostic reporting. If you choose to share diagnostics with developers in your iOS settings, we receive anonymous crash reports that contain no identifying details or custom growth recipe data.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our app does not display advertisements or include third-party trackers. Any external links (such as to this support or policy page) open in your default browser and are subject to their respective terms.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any adjustments will be posted here. Since we do not collect contact details, we recommend checking this page periodically or reviewing updates in the App Store release notes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback about our privacy practices, please contact us at contact.devotellix@gmail.com.
