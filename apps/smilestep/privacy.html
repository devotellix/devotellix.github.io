Security & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

TL;DR: SmileStep is built from the ground up to protect your orthodontic treatment details. All aligner wear logs, progress photos, symptoms, and goal statistics are stored exclusively on your local iOS device. We do not collect, transmit, share, or sell your data.

1. Local-Only & Private Storage

SmileStep operates on an offline-first, local-only model. All created wear logs (including timestamps, active aligner sets, daily notes, and progress photos) are saved locally using Apple's native SwiftData framework. We do not maintain remote servers or databases, and we never transmit your private orthodontic entries over the network.

2. No Account Creation

We do not require user accounts, email registration, logins, or passwords. You can launch the app and start logging your aligner wear time immediately. No personal profiles are created.

3. No Cloud Syncing

SmileStep stores all treatment statistics on the local hardware of your device. Your data remains strictly on your iPhone. There is no automated CloudKit sync, keeping your private medical records isolated and secure.

4. Data Portability & Ownership

You own 100% of your data. Because all data is stored on-device, you have full control over it. If you delete the app, your local logs will be removed by the iOS system. We cannot retrieve or restore your data as we do not have access to it.

5. Zero Ad Scripts or Trackers

SmileStep contains no advertisements, analytics trackers, or external marketing SDKs. We only receive anonymous crash reports from Apple if you have explicitly consented to share diagnostics in your global iOS system settings. These reports contain no personal information or tracking stats.

6. iOS Device Permissions

SmileStep may request the following device permissions to enable specific tracking features:

  • Notifications: Optional. Used to send local alerts reminding you to clean your aligners in the morning, change sets, or put them back in after meals. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not use remote push servers.
  • Camera & Photo Library: Optional. Used to take and save smile progress photos in the app's visual evolution gallery. Photos are processed locally.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as new iOS features or App Store guidelines are introduced. Any updates will be published on this page. Because we do not collect email addresses, we suggest reviewing this policy occasionally or reading the App Store release notes.
