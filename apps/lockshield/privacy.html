Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

TL;DR: LockShield respects your privacy. All radiator layouts, temperature logs, delta-T calculations, valve adjustment records, and reference photos are processed entirely offline. We collect zero personal data, and nothing ever leaves your device.

1. Offline-First Architecture

LockShield is built to run entirely offline on your iOS device. All radiator configurations, layout distances, temperature logs, stabilization rounds, and checklist progress are processed locally using Apple's SwiftData. We do not operate backend servers, and we never sync, transmit, or sell your data.

2. Device Permissions

The application may ask for the following standard iOS permissions depending on how you use it:

  • Camera: Used strictly to allow you to take and attach reference photos of your radiator tés or configuration for quick visual identification. The camera stream is analyzed locally, and the resulting photos are saved directly to your Photo Library or sandbox. We have no access to your camera or photos.
  • Notifications: Used strictly to alert you when your thermal stabilization countdown timer expires (e.g. 30 to 60 minutes between rounds). These alerts are scheduled locally on your device and do not connect to external servers.

3. Local Storage and Backups

Your profiles, checklist status, and customized settings are stored securely within your device's application sandbox. When you back up your iOS device via Apple iCloud, your data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account in accordance with Apple's standard security policies. We do not have access to these backups.

4. Analytics and Crash Logs

To keep LockShield stable and bug-free, we utilize Apple's standard diagnostic reporting. If you choose to share diagnostics with developers in your iOS settings, we receive anonymous crash reports that contain no identifying details or personal heating data.

5. Third-Party Links

Our app does not display advertisements or include third-party trackers. Any external links (such as to energy agency guidelines) open in your default browser and are subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any adjustments will be posted here. Since we do not collect contact details, we recommend checking this page periodically or reviewing updates in the App Store release notes.
