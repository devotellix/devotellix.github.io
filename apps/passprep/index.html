U.S. Photo Quick Rules

Dimensions & Print 2" x 2" (51x51mm)

Must be printed on matte or glossy quality paper. Do not resize or crop digitally.

Eye Wear Rule No Glasses Allowed

Remove eyeglasses, sunglasses, tinted glasses, or lenses, unless medically required (requires statement).

Expression & Background Neutral & White

Smile must be natural, both eyes open. Background must be plain white or off-white.
