Built for the Boot & Leather Enthusiast

A clean utility app with premium dark tones designed to follow the lifetime of your boots.

Wear Tracker

Log daily wears with a single tap. Monitor usage details to know exactly when it is time to brush, clean, or condition your boots.

Smart Drying Timers

Leather needs rest to dry out moisture and maintain structural integrity. Schedule smart drying timers and get notified when your boots are fully rested.

Expert Leather Guides

Step-by-step guides for cleanings, conditionings, and weatherproofing. Tailored instructions for smooth, roughout, suede, and nubuck leathers.

Recommended Products

A curated list of premium conditioners, saddle soaps, and horsehair brushes. Know exactly which products are safe for each specific leather type.

100% Offline Privacy

No account creation, database servers, or third-party trackers. All logs stay safely stored in your local SwiftData container, completely offline.

Collection Insights

Visualize your wear patterns and care statistics. Learn which boots you wear the most and monitor average drying and resting times.

Interactive Boot Care Simulator

Simulate logging boot wears, conditioning your leather, or starting drying timers, and see the interactive preview rendering immediately.
