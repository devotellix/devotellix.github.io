Frequently Asked Questions

  • How are my boots stored? All data (brand, model, wears, conditioning history, and photos) is saved 100% locally on your iOS device via Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no servers and cannot access your database.
  • What does the drying timer do? Leather absorbs sweat and moisture. Resting and drying your boots for at least 24 hours between wears prevents rot, molding, and premature wear. The drying timer keeps track of this resting window.
  • Are there any hidden costs? No. BootWright is 100% free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscription fees.
