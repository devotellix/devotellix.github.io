Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Zero Data Collection

Football 2026: Live Scores is designed with a strict offline-first and privacy-first architecture. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or device information.

2. Local Storage

All app data (including your predictions, quiz scores, and settings) is stored purely locally on your device. It never leaves your device and is never synced to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or analytics platforms. The app operates entirely offline.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. However, our core commitment to zero data collection will never change.
