Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Data Storage & Local Data

Football 2026: Live Scores values your privacy. Your personal selections, predictions, quiz scores, and settings are stored strictly and locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never synced to our servers.

2. Advertisements (Publicités) & Partners

To keep this app free of charge, we display advertisements. These ads are served by third-party advertising partners who may collect and process device-specific information to serve ads that are relevant to you.

Our primary advertising partner is Unity Ads. They may collect device statistics and advertising identifiers to display ads. You can find more information about their data practices here: Unity Privacy Policy.

3. Tracking & Device Identifiers

To provide a personalized advertising experience, we request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt on iOS devices. If authorized, our advertising partners may collect and use device identifiers (such as the IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) and usage statistics to customize the ads shown to you.

If you decline tracking, ads will still be displayed, but they will not be personalized and may be less relevant to you.

4. Analytics & Performance Tracking

We use third-party analytics services to monitor app performance, track crash reports, and gather aggregate usage statistics to improve the app.

Specifically, we use Firebase Analytics (and Google Services) to collect non-personally identifiable logs such as active user counts, session lengths, and app crash details. We also use Firebase Firestore to fetch dynamic configurations like ad frequencies. You can read how Google processes data in their privacy guidelines: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Privacy Controls & Opt-Out

You can manage your tracking preferences and opt-out of personalized advertisements at any time directly through your device settings:

  • On iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and toggle off the tracking permission for this app.
  • On Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads, and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or reset your advertising ID.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact.devotellix@gmail.com
