Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get the best results?

For the most realistic renders, ensure your car is photographed in good lighting (daylight or well-lit garage) and the entire vehicle is visible within the frame. Avoid blurry or heavily filtered images.

What happens when my 25 Premium Images run out?

CarLook Studio includes 25 Ultra HD renders. Once depleted, you can subscribe to our "Extra Images" plan to receive 10 additional renders every month.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly through Apple. Go to the Settings app on your iPhone > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > CarLook Studio to manage or cancel your plan.

Can I use this for my tuning shop?

Yes! CarLook Studio is designed as a B2B sales tool for wrapping studios, wheel dealers, and body shops. You can freely export the Ultra HD before/after renders to use in your client proposals.
