CarLook Studio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Devotellix, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage for the CarLook Studio iOS application.

1. Image Processing & AI Rendering

CarLook Studio requires access to your Photo Library or Camera to function. The photos you select are processed securely by our AI rendering engine to generate the modified vehicle images.

  • Images are temporarily uploaded to our secure servers solely for the purpose of processing the AI render.
  • We do not train generalized public AI models on your personal car photos.
  • Once the Ultra HD render is generated and delivered to your device, the source image and result are deleted from our active processing servers.

2. Data Collection

We believe in minimal data collection. We do NOT use third-party tracking SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks (such as Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or AdMob).

The only data we collect is strictly related to providing the service:

  • Purchase History: Managed entirely by Apple via StoreKit to track your "Extra Images" subscription status and premium credits.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash reports provided by Apple (if you have opted-in via your iOS settings) to help us fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or photos to any third parties under any circumstances.

4. In-App Purchases

All transactions, including the "Extra Images" subscription, are processed securely through Apple's App Store infrastructure. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.

5. Your Rights

Because we do not store your personal data or maintain user accounts, you have complete control over your data. You can revoke Camera and Photo Library permissions at any time via your iOS Settings.
