Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

1. Local Data and Community Votes

Football Fan Hub stores quiz scores, preferences, and settings locally on your device. When you submit a player rating or formation vote, the selected player identifier and vote are sent to Firebase Firestore and combined into anonymous aggregate totals. The app does not require an account and does not attach your name or email address to those votes.

2. Advertisements (Publicités) & Partners

To keep this app free of charge, we display advertisements. These ads are served by third-party advertising partners who may collect and process device-specific information to serve ads that are relevant to you.

Our advertising providers are Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and Pangle. Depending on your consent choices and location, these providers may process device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, ad interactions, and diagnostics. Review their policies: Google, Unity, and Pangle.

3. Tracking & Device Identifiers

To provide personalized advertising, the app requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If authorized, advertising providers may use the iOS advertising identifier (IDFA) and usage information for ad personalization and measurement.

If you decline tracking, ads will still be displayed, but they will not be personalized and may be less relevant to you.

4. Analytics & Performance Tracking

We use third-party analytics services to monitor app performance, track crash reports, and gather aggregate usage statistics to improve the app.

Specifically, we use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and Firebase Firestore to store anonymous aggregate community votes. Information processed may include app interactions, device and app information, diagnostics, and identifiers as described by Google. You can read how Google processes data in its privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Privacy Controls and Retention

You can manage your tracking preferences and opt-out of personalized advertisements at any time directly through your device settings:

  • Tracking: Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and disable permission for Football Fan Hub.
  • Notifications: Manage notification permission in iOS Settings.
  • Local data: Delete the app to remove locally stored quiz scores, preferences, and settings.
  • Community votes: Votes are stored only as aggregate totals and cannot be linked back to an individual user for deletion.

Aggregate community totals are retained while the related feature remains available. Analytics and advertising data retention is controlled by the third-party providers listed above under their respective privacy policies. Contact us with any privacy or deletion request.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact.devotellix@gmail.com
