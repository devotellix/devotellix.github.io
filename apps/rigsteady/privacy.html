RigSteady

Privacy Policy

Effective June 13, 2026

RigSteady does not require an account and does not include advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Local data

Vehicle profiles, calibration settings and campsite logbook entries are stored locally on your device.

Location

Location is requested only when you choose to save a campsite position. Coordinates remain in the local app database and are not transmitted to Devotellix.

Motion sensors

Motion sensor readings are processed on the device to estimate vehicle tilt and are not transmitted or collected.

Contact

Questions may be sent to contact.devotellix@gmail.com.
