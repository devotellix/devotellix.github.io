Frequently Asked Questions

  • How secure is my logged data? Extremely secure. All profiles, discomfort intensities, triggers, and cleaning records are saved locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no servers and cannot access your logs.
  • How do I share data with my dentist? Navigate to the Statistics screen and tap the share button to export a beautifully formatted PDF report of your recent discomfort zones, intensity, and cleaning history.
  • Can I clear my logged history? Yes, you can reset all your data completely in Settings by tapping the "Reset Data" button. This permanently deletes the local SwiftData store.
